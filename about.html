About Hastinapur Paints

Hastinapur Paints is committed to eco-friendly paints that are good for your home and the planet. We focus on natural, scientifically developed formulations that match or outperform conventional paints in quality, durability, and finish.

Our products are lab-tested and engineered for superior coverage, weather resistance, and long life. We offer a wide range of shades – 1000+ vibrant colours – all eco-friendly, non-toxic, and VOC-free. From interior and exterior paints to primers and damp-proof solutions, every product is designed to deliver a professional finish while staying true to our values of sustainability and health.

Eco-friendly paint from cow

Cow dung (or cow-based ingredients) is the main natural ingredient in our eco paint, making it 100% eco-friendly and a genuine alternative to chemical-based paints. Unlike traditional paints, our formulations are scientifically developed and lab-tested so they match or outperform conventional options in quality, durability, and finish – including coverage, weather resistance, and a product life of up to 15 years where applicable. We offer acrylic emulsion eco paint for both interior and exterior use, with Matt, Smooth, or High Sheen finishes.

Our in-house research and development team and dedicated laboratory work on new formulations constantly. Processed cow dung and other natural ingredients are used in a controlled, hygienic process to create stable, durable paint. Our paints contain no harmful chemicals or heavy metals – no arsenic, mercury, chromium, lead, cadmium, or similar toxins. They are VOC-free, non-toxic, and odorless after application, making them safe for homes, schools, and hospitals.

Cow dung is known for its germicidal and natural disinfectant properties, so our paint helps keep walls hygienic and odor-free. Rooted in traditional wisdom and backed by modern science, our paints are safe for children, the elderly, and allergy-sensitive occupants. The coating also acts as a natural thermal insulator, helping to control room temperature. With a wide colour range and uniform, sleek finish, our cow-based paint is cost-effective and sustainable – true Indian innovation for a healthier home.
